New Approach to Modern Education

Our school aims to foster a dynamic learning environment, fostering excellence in academics and behavior, with nurturing teaching, 21st-century skills development, and a sensitive curriculum.

Objectives

To impart sound education under the guidance Of the DMI Sisters. It includes an all-round development spiritual, moral, intellectual, physical, emotional, social and cultural aspects Of the students committed to their care.

Vision

Our Vision is to create global citizens who possess the crucial skills and values to develop a humane society and be the forerunners of thought as responsible citizens of a global community.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide a dynamic learning environment in school that is committed to achieve excellence in academics and behavior in our students. Our school provides a nurturing climate for children, caring teachers to facilitate the development of future skills, and a sensitive curriculum that develops the hearts and minds of our students equally.

system of education

It is a co-education institution with highly qualified staff. It aims in developing the skills and caliber of students in curricular and co-curricular activities.

Sports

The school has regular physical education programme. Coaching facilities are available for athletics, football, yoga and Grate.Every student is encouraged to take active part in sports activities.
•Students who excel in any particular sports are identified and given special coaching under the supervision of our physical Education Teachers.
•The school provides the necessary infrastructure for indoor and outdoor games.

Educational Tour

• "Travelling" is a delightful experience, which keeps the students in tune with the mysteries of nature and the life styles of different people. It broadens their horizon and enriches their education.
•National level sight-seeing tours, one day picnics and short tours are arranged on a regular basis to the places of interest and historical importance. All the students have to participate in any one of the Educational Tours which will be arranged by the Management.
